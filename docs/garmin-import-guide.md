# Importing a FIT Workout to Garmin Connect

After downloading your `.fit` file from Connect Buddy, follow these steps to import it into Garmin Connect.

## Sync to your device

**Via Garmin Connect Mobile (iOS / Android):**
1. Open the Garmin Connect app
2. The workout syncs to your paired device automatically within a few minutes
3. On your device: navigate to **Training → Workouts → My Workouts**

**Via USB (Garmin Express):**
1. Connect your Garmin device via USB
2. Open **Garmin Express**
3. Click **Sync** — workouts are pushed to the device automatically

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| "Import failed" | Verify the `.fit` file was generated by Connect Buddy (not corrupted) |
| Workout not visible on device | Allow 1-2 minutes for Bluetooth sync after importing |
| Wrong sport shown | Check the `sport` field in your workout definition JSON |
| Steps missing | Ensure `num_valid_steps` in the FIT file matches the actual step count |

## Notes

- FIT workout files created by Connect Buddy follow the Garmin FIT Protocol 1.0 / Profile 21.22
- The workout name is truncated to 16 characters on most Garmin devices
- Step names are also limited to 16 characters
